Overview

Voilo makes it effortless to log data into your Google Sheets trackers using natural language. Whether you're tracking expenses, workouts, habits, or any other personal data, simply speak your entry and Voilo will intelligently update your spreadsheet.

Key Features

  • Voice-First Input – speak naturally instead of typing into cells
  • Google Sheets Integration – seamlessly connects with your existing spreadsheets
  • Smart Parsing – automatically understands and structures your spoken data
  • Privacy-Focused – your data stays secure and under your control
  • Time-Saving – log entries in seconds, not minutes

Use Cases

  • Expense tracking and budgeting
  • Fitness and nutrition logging
  • Habit tracking and personal goals
  • Time tracking for projects
  • Daily journaling and notes
  • Inventory management
